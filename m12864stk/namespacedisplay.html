Detailed Description

The panel has 2 F3461BH 8-digit displays. One of them is driven by a 74HCT138, the other is driven by a 74HCT595. Display driver:

A0 1|--—|16 VCC A1 2| U |15 /Y0 A2 3| |14 /Y1 /E1 4| |13 /Y2 /E2 5| |12 /Y3 E3 6| |11 /Y4 /Y7 7| |10 /Y5 GND 8|--—|9 /Y6

A0 - PORTA7 A1 - PORTA6 A2 - PORTA5 E3 - PORTA4 (high active)

Q1 1|--—|16 VCC Q2 2| U |15 Q0 Q3 3| |14 DS Q4 4| |13 /OE Q5 5| |12 STCP Q6 6| |11 SHCP Q7 7| |10 /MR GND 8|--—|9 Q7S

SHCP - PORTA1 STCP - PORTA2 DS - PORTA3
